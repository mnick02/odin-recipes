Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A vanilla milkshake recipe that's very easy to make.

Add chocolate, berries, peaches, or whatever you like. I like to add root beer instead of milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth
  3. Pour into glasses and serve
