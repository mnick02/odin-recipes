Pizza on the Grill

Description

This grilled pizza is topped with tomato sauce, tomatoes, black olives, roasted red peppers, mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil.

Of course, you can use whatever toppings you like! Make sure to serve it with a cold drink and some good tunes!

Ingredients

  • Store-bought pizza dough
  • ¼ cup tomato sauce, divided
  • 1 cup chopped tomatoes, divided
  • ¼ cup sliced black olives, divided
  • ¼ cup roasted red peppers, drained and chopped, divided
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh basil, divided
