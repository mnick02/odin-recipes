Perfect Peanut Butter & Jelly

Description

This is a sandwich made up of a peanut butter and jelly spread on toasted white bread.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • SKIPPY Peanut Butter
  • Welch's grape jelly

Steps

  1. Put two pieces of bread into the toaster
  2. After done toasting, spread Peanut butter on one side
  3. Spread Jelly on the other side and put the two pieces together
